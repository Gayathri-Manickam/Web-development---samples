ELECTRONICS & INSTRUMENTAION ENGINEERING

The Electronics and Instrumentation engineering department established in the year 1994, has been accredited by NBA Tier – I, New Delhi and Institute of Engineers (India), Kolkata. Recognized as Research Centre by Anna University Chennai. Supported by 17 qualified faculty members, among them 5 holds doctoral degree and 7 were pursuing Ph.D. Two funded research projects worth Rs.36 Lakhs from BRNS-IGCAR & ICMR were completed. MSM and DST’S NSTE have sanctioned Rs.10 Lakhs for two student’s projects through NEC Business Incubation and NEC New Gen IEDC.

VISION

  • Producing globally competitive Electronics and Instrumentation Engineers with social responsibilities

MISSION

  • Imparting quality education by providing excellent Teaching-Learning environment.
  • Inculcating qualities of continuous learning, professionalism, team spirit, communication skill and leadership with social responsibilities.
  • Promoting leading edge research and development through collaboration with academia and industry.
