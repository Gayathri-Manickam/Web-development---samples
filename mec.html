MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

The Department came in to being in the year 1984 and initially was steered by late Prof.S.Kannappan. The un-tired efforts of the predecessors had brought in highly integrated and motivated staff strength and an excellent working atmosphere.The department today has comprises of sophisticated laboratory and workshop facilities ensuring on par with standards of global technical education. The department is presently headed by Dr.K.Kalidasa Murugavel. The department, as on date has 35 dedicated and experienced staff members (including 3 adjunct faculties) with 11 doctoral degree holders and the remaining with their PG qualifications.

VISION

  • Producing globally competitive Mechanical Engineers with social responsibilities

MISSION

  • Imparting quality education by providing excellent Teaching-Learning environment.
  • Inculcating qualities of continuous learning, professionalism, team spirit, communication skill and leadership with social responsibilities.
  • Promoting leading edge research and development through collaboration with academia and industry.
