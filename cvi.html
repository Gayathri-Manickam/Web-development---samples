CIVIL ENGINEERING

The Department of Civil Engineering is established in the year 2012 with an intake of 60 students. Presently, it offers under graduate programme in Civil Engineering following own curriculum. The curriculum broadly covers important fields such as Surveying, Geotechnical Engineering, Structural Engineering, Hydraulics and Water Resources Engineering, Construction Materials and Practices, Environmental Engineering and Transportation Engineering.At present, the department is equipped with excellent laboratories such as Surveying Laboratory, Strength of Materials Laboratory, Fluid Mechanics and Machinery Laboratory, Soil Mechanics Laboratory, Civil Engineering Computer aided design and drafting Laboratory, Concrete Laboratory, Highway Engineering Laboratoryand Environmental Engineering Laboratory. The Department has qualified faculty members with excellent academic records.

VISION

  • Producing outstanding civil engineering professionals with human values to face future challenges

MISSION

  • To provide with excellent teaching and research ambience.
  • To prepare student for leadership roles in civil engineering.
  • To facilitate student with lifetime skills and human values.
